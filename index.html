I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science and Engineering at Texas A&M University. My research focuses on medical image segmentation, computer vision, vision-language modeling, and efficient deep learning. I develop robust and practical learning frameworks for real-world applications, with a strong emphasis on healthcare-oriented problems. My broader background includes visual tracking, multimodal learning, and deep neural network design. Before joining Texas A&M, I worked as a researcher at Kyungpook National University and as a software engineer at Samsung R&D Institute Bangladesh.
