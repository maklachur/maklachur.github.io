I am a PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering at Texas A&M University. My research focuses on medical image segmentation, computer vision, vision-language modeling, and efficient deep learning. I develop robust and practical learning frameworks for real-world applications, with a strong interest in healthcare-oriented problems. My broader background includes visual tracking, multimodal learning, and deep neural network design.
